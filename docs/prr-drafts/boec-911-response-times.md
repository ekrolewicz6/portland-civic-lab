Subject: Public records request — monthly 911 call answer and dispatch time data

To the Records Custodian, Bureau of Emergency Communications:

Pursuant to the Oregon Public Records Law (ORS 192.311–192.478), I request
copies of the following records:

Monthly 911 call-taking and dispatch performance data for the period
January 2019 through the present, including, where maintained:

1. Total 911 call volume per month;
2. Call answer time statistics per month, including the percentage of calls
   answered within 20 seconds and median/average answer times;
3. Dispatch time statistics per month by call priority level, where
   available.

I request these records in machine-readable electronic format (CSV or
Excel) if they exist in or can be exported to that form. I believe much of
this data underlies the figures published in the BOEC Director's Reports;
the underlying tabular data, rather than the PDF reports, is what I am
requesting.

I am requesting these records on behalf of Portland Civic Lab, an
independent, non-commercial civic organization that publishes Portland
public data freely at portlandciviclab.org. Because disclosure will be
published openly and primarily benefits the general public, I respectfully
request a fee waiver or reduction under ORS 192.324(5). If fees over $25
will apply, please provide an estimate before proceeding.

If any portion of this request is denied, please cite the specific
exemption claimed and release all reasonably segregable non-exempt
portions.

Thank you for your time. I'm happy to clarify or narrow this request —
the goal is the most useful data with the least burden on your staff.
