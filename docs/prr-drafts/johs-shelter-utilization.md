Subject: Public records request — shelter capacity and utilization data

To the Records Custodian, Joint Office of Homeless Services:

Pursuant to the Oregon Public Records Law (ORS 192.311–192.478), I request
copies of the following records:

Shelter capacity and utilization data for the period January 2022 through
the present, including, where maintained:

1. Nightly or weekly bed/unit capacity by shelter or shelter type
   (congregate, motel, village, etc.);
2. Corresponding occupancy/utilization counts;
3. Turn-away or waitlist counts, where recorded.

I request these records in machine-readable electronic format (CSV or
Excel). I believe this data underlies the Joint Office's public Tableau
dashboard; the underlying tabular extracts, rather than screenshots or
PDFs, are what I am requesting. A recurring export or pointer to a
self-service data source would fully satisfy this request and minimize
staff burden.

I am requesting these records on behalf of Portland Civic Lab, an
independent, non-commercial civic organization that publishes Portland
public data freely at portlandciviclab.org. Because disclosure will be
published openly and primarily benefits the general public, I respectfully
request a fee waiver or reduction under ORS 192.324(5). If fees over $25
will apply, please provide an estimate before proceeding.

If any portion of this request is denied, please cite the specific
exemption claimed and release all reasonably segregable non-exempt
portions.

Thank you — happy to clarify or narrow this request.
